Services

At Celestial Health Solutions, we provide comprehensive, patient-centered care for individuals and families. Our goal is to support your overall health and wellness through a wide range of services, delivered with expertise, compassion, and convenience.

Primary Care

Our primary care services focus on prevention, early detection, and management of chronic conditions. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to managing diabetes, hypertension, and other health concerns, we partner with you to keep your health on track.

Wound Care

We offer advanced wound care for both acute and chronic wounds, including diabetic ulcers, pressure sores, and post-surgical wounds. Our team uses evidence-based treatments and personalized care plans to promote faster healing and prevent complications.

Behavioral & Mental Health

Your mental health matters. Our behavioral and mental health services provide evaluation, therapy, and ongoing support for conditions such as depression, anxiety, and stress-related disorders. We are committed to helping you achieve emotional well-being and balance.

Telemedicine

We offer secure telemedicine visits, you can connect with your provider virtually from home, work, or on the go. Quick, convenient, and confidential care right at your fingertips.

Women’s Health

We provide comprehensive care tailored to women, including annual exams, reproductive health, menopause management, and preventive screenings. Our team prioritizes your comfort and well-being at every stage of life.

Laboratory

Our in-house laboratory testing ensures accurate and timely results for a wide range of health needs, including blood work, metabolic panels, and other routine labs. Quick access to lab results allows us to make informed decisions for your care.

Diagnositc Testing

We offer essential diagnostic tests to help identify and monitor health conditions:

  • EKGs (Electrocardiograms): Evaluate heart rhythm and detect heart conditions
  • PFTs (Pulmonary Function Tests): Assess lung function and respiratory health
  • ABIs (Ankle-Brachial Tests): Assess blood flow in legs and detect peripheral artery disease
  • Other diagnostic screenings as needed to support comprehensive care
