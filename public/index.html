Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

Celestial Health Solutions offers TMS therapy, a safe, non-invasive, and FDA-cleared treatment for depression and other mood disorders. TMS uses gentle magnetic pulses to stimulate specific areas of the brain, helping patients who may not have responded adequately to medications.
